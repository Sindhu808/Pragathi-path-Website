Career Roadmap

To pursue a career in this field, women should begin with fundamental knowledge through government agriculture training programs, followed by gaining hands-on experience through internships or working on local farms. Next, certifications in specific domains like organic farming, seed processing, or livestock management add credibility. Once confident, starting small-scale ventures using available land or collaborating with other women in the region helps in managing risk. Government schemes, microfinance, and NGOs can offer financial and technical assistance. As the venture grows, they can explore branding, value addition, and marketing via digital platforms to increase their income and reach wider markets. Community engagement, continuous learning, and networking with experts through mentorship programs ensure long-term success.
