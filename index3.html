Pickle & Papad Making

Pickle Making Steps

  • Choose Ingredients – Mango, lemon, garlic, etc.
  • Prepare – Wash, dry, and cut.
  • Spice Mix – Use traditional Indian spices.
  • Mix & Marinate – Blend well and let it sit.
  • Storage – Use airtight jars and let it mature.

Papad Making Steps

  • Prepare Dough – With lentil flour and spices.
  • Knead & Rest – Make stiff dough.
  • Roll & Dry – Thin out and sun-dry.
  • Store & Cook – Fry or roast before eating.

Homemade Bakery & Catering

Homemade Bakery Steps

  • Choose Your Specialty – Cakes, cookies, etc.
  • Gather Equipment – Oven, mixer, tools.
  • Perfect Recipes – Refine and standardize.
  • Legal & Branding – Register and package smartly.
  • Marketing – Use digital and local channels.

Catering Business Steps

  • Define Your Menu – Focus your service.
  • Source Ingredients – Find reliable vendors.
  • Setup Kitchen – Prepare for bulk orders.
  • Customer Service – Delight clients and scale.

Dairy & Egg Production

Dairy Production Steps

  • Choose the Right Breed – Based on climate.
  • Farm Setup – Maintain hygiene and comfort.
  • Nutrition – Balanced diet and supplements.
  • Milking & Storage – Follow hygienic practices.
  • Marketing – Sell through markets or cooperatives.

Egg Production Steps

  • Select Breed – Choose high-yield layers.
  • Housing – Provide safe, airy coops.
  • Feeding & Care – Ensure nutrition and health.
  • Collect & Store – Store in a cool, dry space.
  • Sales – Reach out to local buyers.
